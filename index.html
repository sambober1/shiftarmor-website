The Solution

Six integrated modules working together to keep your operation running safely and efficiently.

Intelligent Scheduling

Drag-and-drop shifts with automatic conflict detection, overtime alerts, and policy enforcement. Create a week's schedule in minutes, not hours.

  • Multiple views: week, day, list, timeline
  • Bulk shift creation with recurring patterns
  • Draft-to-publish workflow
  • Real-time conflict prevention

Employee Management

Centralized profiles with certification tracking, training status, and compliance badges. Know who's qualified for every task.

  • Bulk CSV import for fast onboarding

  • Active/inactive status management

Incident Reporting

OSHA-aligned digital forms with photo attachments, root cause analysis, and automatic 300 log tracking. Replace paper with compliance-ready records.

  • OSHA 300/301/300A ready
  • Severity and recordability tracking
  • Work restriction integration
  • Trend analysis dashboards

Training Matrix

Program library, session scheduling, and enrollment tracking. Never miss a certification renewal or let an unqualified worker on the floor.

  • Mandatory, certification, equipment types
  • Session scheduling with capacity
  • Automatic expiration alerts
  • Scheduling integration

Access Control

  • Owner → Admin → Manager hierarchy
  • Custom role creation
  • Complete audit trails
  • Multi-tenant isolation
